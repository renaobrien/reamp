    About the equalizer

    Reamp is remote-controlling Spotify or Music, and macOS does not let one app reshape another app's audio. So for now the EQ and balance sliders are visual only.

    A real equalizer arrives with API mode, where Reamp plays the music itself and can process the sound before it reaches your speakers. The visuals already follow the true audio through the capture helper.
